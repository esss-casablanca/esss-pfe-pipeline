# Submission Pack Template (per journal)

Each of the three packs contains:

1. **Formatted manuscript** (`.docx`) — V5 reformatted to this journal's instructions for authors,
   science unchanged. Name it clearly, e.g. `Manuscript_<JournalShortName>.docx`.
2. **Cover-letter draft** (`.docx`) — addressed to the editor, stating the review's contribution, fit
   with the journal's scope, confirmation of originality and non-simultaneous submission, and the
   corresponding author. Mark personalisation points `[AUTHOR TO VERIFY]`; the student must finalise
   it and obtain supervisor approval.
3. **Submission checklist** (`.docx` or `.md`) — the journal's required items (manuscript sections,
   declarations, reporting-guideline checklist, figures/tables, suggested reviewers if requested),
   plus every outstanding `[... REQUIRED]` marker the student must resolve before submitting.

## Reminder to include in every pack
- Submit to **one** journal at a time; the three packs are ranked alternatives.
- Resolve all `[... REQUIRED]` markers and obtain supervisor sign-off before submission.
