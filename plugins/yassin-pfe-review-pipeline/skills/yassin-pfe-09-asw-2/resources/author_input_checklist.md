# Author Input Checklist

Use this checklist when revision cannot be finalized without author or supervisor confirmation.

## Study identity

- What is the exact title?
- What is the study design?
- What is the target population?
- What is the field/setting?
- Is this a student project, mémoire, article, protocol, or note de cadrage?

## Methods

- What was/will be the sampling strategy?
- What are the inclusion and exclusion criteria?
- What is the expected or actual sample size?
- What tool/instrument/questionnaire was/will be used?
- Was the tool validated or adapted?
- How and when were/will data be collected?
- What statistical or qualitative analysis was/will be used?

## Ethics

- Is ethics approval required/obtained?
- Was informed consent obtained/planned?
- How is anonymity/confidentiality protected?
- Are there sensitive participants or clinical data?

## Results and interpretation

- What are the main findings?
- Which tables/figures support them?
- Which findings should be emphasized?
- Which claims must remain cautious?

## References

- Which references must be preserved?
- Which claims need additional references?
- Are references real and verified?

## Final verification

- Does the revised text preserve your intended meaning?
- Are all markers resolved?
- Are there any claims you cannot defend orally?
- Does the text comply with supervisor, journal, or institutional expectations?
